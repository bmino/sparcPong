Challenges

Issuing Challenges

  • Issue a challenge from the Singles Board or Doubles Board.
  • Click the "Challenge" button to issue a challenge.

Completing and Revoking Challenges

  • Report a challenge from your Profile or the profile of your opponent.
  • Click the "Report" button to report a challenge that has been completed.
  • The winner or the loser can report the results.

Forfeiting Challenges

  • Forfeiting a challenge can be done from your Profile or the profile of your opponent.
  • Only the challengee can forfeit.
