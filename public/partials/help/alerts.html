Alerts

Overview

Configure your alert settings to get notifications for important events such as being challenged. Alerts help you focus on your daily work and bring sparc pong into your focus when relevant.

Setting up Alerts

Before any alerts will be sent, you must have an email address on file. This email address can be a regular run of the mill email address, or the mail server for your wireless company. Configuring your email address this way will allow you to receive text message alerts on your mobile device. Log in and navigate to the "Account" tab to add an email. For example, (843) 123-4567 on a verizon network would be 8431234567@vtext.com

Configuring Alerts

Now that you have an email address on file, you might not want to receive alerts for everything. Log in and navigate to the "Account" tab. Here you can toggle alerts for the categories listed. The default is to receive challenge and forfeit alerts.

  • Challenged - alerted when someone challenges you
  • Revoked - alerted when someone revokes a challenge against you
  • Resolved - alerted when you are involved in a completed challenge
  • Forfeited - alerted when someone forfeits your issued challenge
