Game Rules

Prerequisites

An official match is put in motion by issuing a challenge through the ladder system. Verbal challenges are great, but to advance on the ladder those words must be put into code! See the Challenges guide for more information on how to do this and to report the outcome of the match.

Beginning a Match

  • PING to determine who will serve first.
  • While not required, please remind your opponent this is a challenge match.

Serving

  • Present the ball flat above your palm at table level.
  • Toss the ball at least six inches without spinning the ball.
  • Strike the ball behind the table edge.
  • The ball must bounce once on your side and at least once on the opponent's side.
  • If the ball meets the above conditions AND contacts the net, redo the serve.
  • While not required, it is good practice to declare the score before serving.

Switching Serves

  • Alternate service player every two points.
  • When both players have 10+ points a different serving pattern occurs.
    • The player who served at 0-0 will always serve when the score is equal.
    • The other player will serve at all other times.

Winning a Game

  • The first player to have 11+ points and be ahead by 2 points is the winner.

Winning the Match

  • Best of 5 games when challenging tier 1.
  • Best of 3 games when challenging tier 2+.
